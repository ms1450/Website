About Me

QUALIFICATIONS | SKILLS | ACHIEVEMENTS

As an M.S. Cybersecurity graduate from the Rochester Institute of Technology and Solutions Engineer at Verkada, I bring a strong technical foundation and hands-on experience in both cybersecurity and physical security. My background spans programming, machine learning applications, and networking, all crucial for safeguarding diverse, cloud-managed systems.
In my role as a Network Support Technician Intern at Wegmans Food Markets, I managed and optimized large network infrastructures while developing tools for improved network configuration management. My experience as a Security Operations Center Intern at Value Point Tech further refined my expertise in SOC operations, including effective use of SIEM and SOAR platforms.
Currently certified in CompTIA Security+ and Network+, I have a deep understanding of cryptography, network security (including routing and switching, IDS/IPS, and threat modeling), and hardware security. My technical proficiency extends to penetration testing, reverse engineering, and cloud-managed security solutions. I also provide technical expertise to customers and partners, design optimal security systems, and engage in scripting and programming at Verkada.
I remain committed to continuous learning, having completed a machine learning specialization via Coursera and a hands-on externship with AT&T. These experiences, combined with my current role, prepare me to contribute meaningfully to the evolving field of cybersecurity and enterprise security. Please feel free to get in touch!
